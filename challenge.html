The Challenge

Accepting the challenge to participate in the GUEP, Monroe County has done the following:

  • Established an organizing body called the Monroe County Energy Challenge Task Force (with a link for more details on the task force)
  • Developed an Energy Plan which describes how our community will become more energy efficient and how we intend to comply with the requirements of the GUEP (with a link to “THE PLAN”)
  • Secured the support and participation of all applicable utility partners (link here to Utility Partners)
  • Enlisted the support of many public and private schools (link here to participating schools)
  • Engaged a group of fantastic volunteers to implement the Energy Plan
