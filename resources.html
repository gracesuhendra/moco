Rebates and Other Financial Incentives


State

See http://www.in.gov/oed/2379.htm

  • Up to $1,000 deduction for INSTALLING Solar-powered roof vent or fan (see Indiana Department of Revenue’s Information Bulletin #105)
  • Up to $1,000 deduction for UPGRADING (not just replacing) insulation (storm windows, storm doors, included) on older houses. There are also credits for alternative energy. (See Indiana Department of Revenue’s Information Bulletin #43).
  • Property tax deductions available on solar, wind & geothermal installations. (Go to http://www.in.gov/dlgf/2344.htm and look for "Deduction Forms" at the bottom of the page.)

Federal

through December 31, 2016

Federal tax credit for 30% of the installed cost, up to $500 per .5 KW of power capacity for Solar Panels.
